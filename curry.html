Description

Curry is an intensely flavorful,aromatic dish featuring meat,fish, or vegetables cooked in a sauce seasoned with a complex blend of spices like turmeric,coriander and cumin.

  • Boneless Chicken (thighs or breast)
  • Yogurt (or Hung Curd/Greek Yogurt for marinade)
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste (or fresh grated ginger and garlic)
  • Butter (essential for the dish)
  • Heavy Whipping Cream or Fresh Cream
  • Tomatoes (puree, crushed, or fresh)
  • Butter Chicken Masala powder (store-bought mix) OR a blend of individual spices:
    • Garam Masala
    • Kashmiri Chili Powder (for color) or Red Chili Powder
    • Coriander Powder
    • Turmeric Powder
    • Cumin Powder (optional)
  • Kasuri Methi (dried fenugreek leaves) - often crushed
  • Oil or Ghee
  • Onion (chopped or grated - sometimes omitted in authentic versions)
  • Cashews (or blanched almonds for creaminess)
  • Whole Spices (e.g., Cinnamon, Cardamom, Cloves, Bay Leaf) - for the gravy base
  • Salt
  • Sugar (to balance the acidity of the tomatoes)
  • Lemon Juice (for the marinade)

Steps to prepare Butter Chicken Masala

  1. Marinate the Chicken:
    • Cut the boneless chicken into bite-sized pieces.
    • Combine yogurt/curd, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, and a mix of spices (Kashmiri chili powder, garam masala, turmeric, salt).
    • Coat the chicken thoroughly in the marinade and refrigerate for a minimum of 30 minutes, or preferably several hours/overnight.
  2. Cook the Chicken:
    • Heat oil or a mix of oil and butter in a pan, skillet, or grill.
    • Cook the marinated chicken pieces until they are browned and nearly cooked through (they will finish cooking in the sauce).
    • Remove the cooked chicken from the pan and set it aside.
  3. Prepare the Makhani Gravy Base:
    • In the same pan (or a new pot), melt butter and add whole spices (like cinnamon stick, cardamom pods, and cloves) until fragrant.
    • Add chopped onions (optional) and saut√© until soft and translucent.
    • Add ginger-garlic paste and powdered spices (like Kashmiri chili powder, coriander, and turmeric). Cook for about one minute until fragrant.
    • Add tomato puree/crushed tomatoes, salt, and raw cashew nuts (for thickness and creaminess).
    • Simmer the mixture until the tomatoes break down and the oil starts to separate.
  4. Blend and Strain the Gravy:
    • Remove the whole spices (optional, but recommended for a smooth texture).
    • Transfer the cooked gravy mixture to a blender and blend until completely smooth.
    • For a restaurant-style smoothness: Pass the blended gravy through a fine-mesh strainer back into the cooking pot, discarding any solids left in the strainer.
  5. Simmer and Finish:
    • Bring the strained gravy to a gentle simmer.
    • Add sugar (to balance the acidity) and a generous pinch of crushed **Kasuri Methi** (dried fenugreek leaves).
    • Add the pre-cooked chicken pieces and simmer for 5-10 minutes to allow the chicken to absorb the sauce.
    • Stir in the heavy cream and a final knob of butter (this is the "butter" part of the butter chicken!).
    • Taste and adjust salt, sugar, or spice level as needed.
  6. Serve:
    • Garnish with fresh cilantro and a swirl of cream.
    • Serve hot with Naan, Roti, or Basmati Rice.
